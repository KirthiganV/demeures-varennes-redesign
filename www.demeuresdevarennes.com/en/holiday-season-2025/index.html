Holidays season

In the heart of Ile-de-France

For the holiday season, make Les Demeures de Varennes your country retreat. A place where you’ll love to gather when winter arrives: comfortable rooms, a soothing spa, a welcoming restaurant, shared laughter, and leisurely breakfasts that linger after the festive celebrations…

Feel at home

At Les Demeures de Varennes, you can unpack your bags as if you were in a family home for the holidays.
Take the time to relax, reconnect as a couple or with your family, enjoy a cozy stay, and welcome the new year in a warm and peaceful atmosphere.

Gathering around the table

Throughout the holiday season, La Table de Varennes invites you to share and enjoy convivial moments around a heartfelt and comforting seasonal cuisine:

A menu featuring the flavors of winter
Christmas Brunch, December 25 (€45 per person)
Christmas Eve Dinner, December 24 (€95 per person)
New Year’s Eve Dinner, December 31 (€159 per person), followed by an evening of music and dancing

Take a break

Between festive occasions, take a moment to unwind.
The Nuxe Spa at Les Demeures de Varennes welcomes you for a wellness retreat, ideal for relaxing, recharging, and ending the year gently, like in a country house where you can let yourself be carried away by the calm of winter.
