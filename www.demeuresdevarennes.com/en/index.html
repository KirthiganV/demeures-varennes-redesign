The quaint tranquillity
of a
country home

Les Demeures de Varennes **** – BW Signature Collection by Best Western welcomes you to a peaceful, verdant estate at the entrance to the village of Varennes-Jarcy. Nestled within a five-acre wooded park, whose century-old gardens date back to 1740, the 18th-century manor house, the longère (traditional Breton farmhouse), and the various other residences perpetuate the charm of a refined country home.

The main building has been beautifully renovated, now offering a sublime and modern setting while preserving its historical character.

The atmosphere, inspired by the world of Gustave Caillebotte, pays homage to the light, landscapes, and art of living so dear to the Impressionists, offering an authentic escape just outside Paris.

The estate offers 66 rooms, including 13 located in the heart of the Castle, as well as 7 rooms, including 2 newly furnished, to accommodate both your seminars and your receptions: weddings, baptisms, birthdays and other precious moments to celebrate in a place full of charm.

The Nuxe Spa extends this serene atmosphere in a space entirely dedicated to well-being, where nature-inspired treatments invite profound relaxation.

La Table de Varennes has taken up residence at the Castel, in a bright, glass-roofed room open to nature. A welcoming bar also provides a space for sharing moments with family or friends.

Nestled between the Sénart Forest and the Brie region, in this area known as “The Gardens of Île-de-France,” Les Demeures de Varennes cultivates the spirit of traditional country houses: a secret, soothing, and inspiring place where time seems to stand still, like in an Impressionist painting.

A sweet moment at Les Demeures de Varennes: Brunch & NUXE Spa

Every Sunday, come enjoy our delicious Sunday brunch and enjoy free access to the NUXE Spa at Les Demeures de Varennes. An enchanting interlude where indulgence and relaxation meet.

Brunch served every Sunday from 12 p.m.:

  • Continental breakfast with local products
  • Pear and cinnamon crumble
  • Smoked salmon, various terrines, and eggs Benedict
  • Assortment of sweet desserts and mature cheeses
  • Hot drinks and fruit juices included

Your complimentary access to the NUXE Spa, valid with any brunch reservation, includes access to our wellness area: hammam, sauna, sensory shower, and herbal tea area (subject to availability).

For absolute relaxation, complete your experience by booking a NUXE treatment or discovering our signature treatment at Les Demeures de Varennes: a unique sensory invitation, combining gentle botanicals and deep relaxation.

Every Sunday from 12 p.m. €45 per person, €20 for children under 12.
